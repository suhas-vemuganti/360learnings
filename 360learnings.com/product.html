Products

No one understands the technology behind a successful learning initiative better than us. This expertise has helped us build products that are cutting-edge, robust, scalable and easy to deploy.

360° Learning System

Our Learning Management System fits neatly into your learning environment providing a sturdy backbone to all your learning initiatives. We have followed the Competency-based learning model to develop our LMS, as competency-based learning is clearly the preferred way to learn. Our LMS is built to map people competencies to their learning needs. This way the LMS can create individual development plans based on existing skills and competencies. 360º Learning System is AICC/SCORM compliant.

360° Explore

360º Explore is an assessment engine framework that helps you assess and evaluate the skill levels of a candidate. An ideal product that will help you screen and recruit the right candidates into your organization. A secure client can be used in a proctored environment. Explore supports a variety of question types including: Fill in the Blank, Multiple Choice, True/False, Yes/No etc.
